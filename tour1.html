About the tour

Brave souls gather! In our seasonal special, we bring you the the bizarre, the weird and the plain freaky to some. This is not a tour for the faint-hearted, so don't say we never warned you. When the sun sets, some of Singapore's best supper cuisine comes to live. Expect a taste of local supper favourites such as the Milo "Godzilla", frog porridge or even crocodile paws! You'll also get a glimpse of Singapore's supper scene and behind the scenes of our hardworking chefs that keep the bellies of Singaporeans satisfied in the wee hours of the night. So if you're feeling hungry and adventurous, come join us on this limited time seasonal tour now!
