About Us

Our Humble Beginnings

Back then, who would have known that an idea between 4 food khakis, over a meal of Singaporean delights, would have blossomed into one of Singapore’s largest food tour groups. We started WokSG with the goal of introducing visitors to the vibrant local food scene and have since been firing up over 100 food tours monthly for our visitors.

Established in 2015, we have grown our team to over 30 staff and local food guides today. We work in collaboration with more than 80 food vendors and partners to bring you the best Singaporean flavours. Our team of friendly guides will take you to these eateries and food shops beloved by the locals, and we know you will fall in love with them too.
