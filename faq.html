FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

During booking

What are the discount packages available?

Currently, we have 3 different discount packages for you to choose from: Family discounts, Student deals, and Local foodie goodies. Learn more here.

What if I have dietary requirements?

If you have any dietary requirements, please indicate them in the "Remarks" section of the booking form.

Can I book a private tour?

Yes! Please contact us either at support@woksg.com or through the form located on our Contact Us page at least 10 working days in advance. A minimum of 5 is required to start a private tour.

Before the tour

What should I bring for the tour?

On the description page for each tour, we have a list of recommended items under the section "What should I bring?".

Can I pull out of a tour?

If you wish to pull out of a tour (we recommend for you not to), please let us know at least 7 days in advance. You may contact us either at support@woksg.com or through the form located on our Contact Us page.

During the tour

Will there be a lot of walking?

Although we provide transport for travel between longer distances, some walking is expected in the course of the tour.

How big is a tour group?

Around 5-15 pax.

How long does a tour last?

Our tour durations range from 3 hours for a short tour to 8 hours for a day tour.

Can I join or leave a tour halfway?

We recommend that you stay for the whole tour duration to get the full WokSG experience, however if you are joining or leaving us midway please do let the guides know!
