About the tour

In this tour, you'll get to experience the authentic Singaporean cuisine - a melting pot of interesting flavours and tastes from different ethnic groups and cultures. From the savoury Katong Laksa to the Punggol Power Nasi Lemak, we'll keep your taste buds tingling with excitement. You'll also get to learn more about Singapore's hawker heritage that has become intrinsic to the Singaporean identity. But that's not all, we'll also kick it up a notch with some of our secret specials in the tour. Hop on the tour with our friendly guides and immerse yourself in the "shiok" experience today!

What to bring

A sense of adventure and love for good food
A stomach that's ready to be satisfied

What to expect

A friendly tour guide to bring you around
Lots of sights, sounds and tastes unique only to Singapore
